Our Foundation

INTRODUCTION

Unemployment,poverty,insecurity and social decay amongst others are pressing issues in Nigeria, Africa and across the globe. These challenge create complex social, economic and environmental obstacles that hinder progress,prosperity and destiny actualization Nigeria, for example, people strive daily with hope for a brighter future,despite the persistent difficulties they face.

On the other hand,information and knowledge are widely recognized as crucial tools for human,moral,physical,mental and social development. However,the quality of information accessed by many individuals,as well as the right attitude needed to nurture both their minds and inherent potentials are insufficient,impactful figures like Aliko Dangote,Wole Soyinka and Ngozi Okonjio-Iweala (to mention a few)have achieved remarkable success largely due to the quality of information they accessed by their determination succeed

We are Eagle Eye with Debbie (EED) believe that greatness is not determined by one's background,environment,gender,social status or religion rather by an individual willingness to discover self and undergo personal development.
